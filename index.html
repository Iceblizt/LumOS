What is LumOS?

Redefining Office Illumination

LumOS is an intelligent IoT ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between energy efficiency and human comfort. By integrating mmWave presence detection with real-time power monitoring, LumOS ensures lights are active only when needed.

More than just a light switch, LumOS provides detailed usage analytics via a built-in TFT dashboard and offers seamless control through a mobile application.

Why LumOS?
